Our Story

From Nature to Your Skin

Founded in 2010, Lumière began as a small family-owned business with a passion for natural skincare. Our founder, Isabella Rose, believed that what you put on your skin should be as pure as what you put in your body.

Today, we continue to handcraft our products in small batches to ensure quality and freshness. Each product is infused with botanical extracts and essential oils that work in harmony with your skin.

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond our ingredients to our packaging, which uses recycled materials and is fully recyclable.
